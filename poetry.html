Poems and Writings

Poems and Writings By Sujeeth Gorati

Happy Fathers day!

As,I imitate your actions,
Mom expresses her satisfaction.
Through her eyes because of affection,
Imitating your actions makes me proud,
Missing your absence makes me vulnerable,
Which makes me kind
When you cross my mind.

-Sujeeth Gorati
I smile at windy clouds,
As moisture in the air,
Smashes my body hair
My eyes blink as I breathe deeply,
I feel peace with relieved smile on face.

-Sujeeth Gorati
If you got to go,
I'll let you go,
By not letting anyone to visit,
As,I reached my limit

-Sujeeth Gorati
His eyes fell in love with her chaos,
With confused eyes on her,
With disturbed heart beat in chest,
She calms down her chaos with smile,
As,she spots his eyes on her

-Sujeeth Gorati
When you started to care,
I thought we were a match,
We may not endup as a pair,
But,we're not supposed to detach.

-Sujeeth Gorati
I write my pain and pleasure,
To heal and entertain not to complain,
By spilling it out through ink,
By healing what I overthink

-Sujeeth Gorati
My heart is feeling better now,
As,its broken pieces feel peace,
By settling down together now ,
You made my heart and soul healthy,
Even though you left me.

-Sujeeth Gorati
